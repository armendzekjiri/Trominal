FAQ

Before you ask.

Is there a paid version?

No. There is no Pro tier, no Enterprise tier, no feature gates. The full app is MIT-licensed.

Do you offer hosted Trominal?

A small "Trominal Cloud" beta exists for hobbyists who don't want to run their own server. It's optional and can be turned off in one click. Full data export is built in.

Can I get support for my company?

Two maintainers offer paid consulting / priority support — see the support page. The community Discord is free and active.

Where are you legally based?
