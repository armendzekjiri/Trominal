  • Bastion / jump host chains, multi-hop
  • Connection-state badges across the team

Trominal AI

An AI that reads your terminal — not a chat bot.

Inline ghost-text suggestions appear after your last command and the output it produced. Tab to accept, escape to dismiss. No popups, no chat windows, no asking ChatGPT what your error means.

  • Choose your provider — Anthropic, OpenAI, or your own LLM endpoint
  • Per-host opt-in for sending terminal context
  • Redaction rules — auto-strip secrets before they leave
  • Local-first mode for fully offline use
Mobile

A keyboard built for the shell, not Slack.

Two terminal-tuned symbol rows put pipe, tilde, slash, redirects, and angle brackets one tap from your thumb. Above the keys: snippet quick-bar, history scroll, and an AI button.

  • Modifier row with ctrl, esc, ^C, ^D
  • Arrow cluster with history-up shortcut
  • Snippet bar reorderable per-host
  • Swipe up for emoji / 123 / system layouts

And the rest

The full feature set.

SFTP browser

Drag-drop transfers, mass rename, parallel uploads. Works on slow connections.

Port forwarding

Local, remote, and SOCKS5 dynamic. Toggle from any device, traffic stats included.

Snippets & templates

Parameterized commands shared across the team. Mustache-style variables.

Identities & certs

ed25519, ECDSA, RSA, certificates. Per-host overrides. Hardware-key support.

Team workspaces

Roles, audit log, single-sign-on via OIDC. Granular per-host permissions.

GPU-accelerated render

WGPU-backed terminal. 120fps scrollback, smooth even on huge log dumps.

Git-native sync

Optional: back your config with a Git repo. PR your firewall rules like code.

Universal search

⌘K finds hosts, snippets, files, scrollback, even past sessions.

Themable

Five built-in palettes plus full custom themes. Sync them across the team too.

Deliberately absent

What Trominal does not do.

Bloat is a feature decision. We say no a lot.

×
No telemetry
Zero analytics, zero usage pings, zero "anonymous" identifiers.
×
No third-party clouds
Your data is on your server. Period. No "optional" sync to ours.
×
No enterprise paywall
SAML, audit log, RBAC — all in the OSS build. Forever.
×
No agent on your servers
Trominal speaks plain SSH. No daemons, no sidecars, no auto-installers.
